# Premium Summaries

## Biology: Evolution
- **Natural Selection**: Evolutionary process where organisms better adapted to their environment tend to survive and produce more offspring. Introduced by Charles Darwin.

## History: World War II
- **D-Day (June 6, 1944)**: The Allied invasion of Normandy in Operation Overlord, marking a turning point against Nazi Germany in WWII.
- **Pearl Harbor (Dec 7, 1941)**: A surprise attack by Japan on the U.S. naval base that led to America entering WWII.
